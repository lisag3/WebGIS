Heat Vulnerability of NYC Neighborhoods

How to use this map
Choose a background layer and select visibility of data layers from the layers control box in the upper right. The most recently selected layer is shown on top. Click on water features points for more information. Hover over Surface Temps layer for information on each neighborhood. Use the search box to search for neighborhoods by name.

Heat Vulnerability Index is a score from 1 to 5, with 5 being the most vulnerable to severe heat events.

Surface temperatures are in degrees Fahrenheit.
